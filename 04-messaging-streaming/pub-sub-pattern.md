# Pub/Sub Pattern

## Introduction

**Publish/Subscribe** broadcasts messages to multiple subscribers. Producers publish to topics; subscribers receive independently. Enables event-driven, loosely coupled architectures.

## Definition

| Concept | Definition |
|---------|------------|
| **Pub/Sub** | Publish to topic; multiple subscribers receive |
